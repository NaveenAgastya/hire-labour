How does the platform work?

WorkForce Connect is a platform that connects customers with skilled workers for various labor services. You can browse available services, book workers based on your needs, and pay securely through our platform. All workers are verified and rated by previous customers.

Are the workers insured?

Yes, all bookings through our platform include insurance coverage. Our workers are covered for accidents and potential damages that might occur during the job, giving you peace of mind when hiring through WorkForce Connect.

How much notice do I need to give for booking?

You can book as soon as same-day service depending on worker availability. We recommend booking at least 24-48 hours in advance for the best selection of workers. For large projects or multiple workers, 3-5 days notice is ideal.

What if I need to cancel my booking?

You can cancel your booking up to 24 hours before the scheduled time for a full refund. Cancellations with less than 24 hours notice will incur a 50% cancellation fee. This policy helps ensure our workers are compensated fairly for their scheduled time.

How are workers vetted?

All workers undergo thorough background checks, identity verification, and skill assessment before joining our platform. We also maintain a rating system based on customer feedback to ensure ongoing quality service. Only workers who maintain a minimum rating are allowed to continue on the platform.

Do workers bring their own tools?

This depends on the service and your specific requirements. You can specify whether you want workers to bring tools when you book. Some services automatically include basic tools, while others may require you to provide equipment. This will be clearly indicated during the booking process.
